# Project C: "UniDine" (Mobile Cafeteria Ordering)

## Context
The lunch rush at the main cafeteria is chaotic. Lines are 20 minutes long, and students with allergies often hold up the line asking about ingredients.

## Raw Elicitation Notes
1. Cafeteria Manager: "We need to reduce the queue. I want students to order food on their phone while they are walking from class. The kitchen needs a screen that shows orders as they come in."
2. Interview with Chef: "I need to be able to mark an item as Sold Out instantly. Yesterday, people kept ordering the vegan wrap for 10 minutes after we ran out. It was a nightmare."
3. Student with Nut Allergy: "I need to filter the entire menu. If I check No Peanuts, I do not even want to see the peanut butter cookie on the screen. It gives me anxiety."
4. IT Infrastructure Team: "The cafeteria basement has terrible Wi-Fi. The kitchen display system needs to be able to work offline for at least 10 minutes without crashing if the internet cuts out. It should sync up once the connection is back."
5. Marketing Dept: "We want to send push notifications to students at 11:30 AM offering them a discount on the daily special."
